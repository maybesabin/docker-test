Every batch, five stages

The roast, stage by stage

01 · 0:00

Charge

Green beans drop into a preheated 200°C drum, temperature dips as they absorb heat.

02 · 1:40

Dry end

Moisture leaves the bean, it turns from green to a pale straw yellow.

03 · 6:15

Maillard

Sugars and amino acids brown the bean, this window builds most of the flavor.

04 · 9:42

First crack

Steam pressure splits the bean audibly. We taste and adjust airflow from here on.

05 · 11:30

Drop & cool

Beans hit the cooling tray at 205°C and are air-cooled in under 4 minutes.
